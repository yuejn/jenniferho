Hi, I'm Jennifer! I'm an Amsterdam-based developer who likes to write about startups and take photographs. I have experience sharing bathrooms with monkeys and taking projects from napkin to MVP-stage, in that order.*

I'm fascinated by technologies that better our lives, and have a soft spot for those that improve access to our cultural histories.

Recent ventures have included building a smart contact management bot called Mila and a meetup platform for students at the University of Leiden. I was the first hire in both companies. I've also worked on projects related to publishing, information management, and travel security.

* = I programmed out of Bali for a few months where monkeys were a local hazard. I do not recommend showing your fear.
